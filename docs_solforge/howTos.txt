1) creating a token / launch
  goto https://dapp.solforgeai.com
  click "Forge a New token"
    Enter Name, Ticker, Description and upload a logo for your token
  option 1: you can click the socials button and include
      Twitter
      website
      telegram
  option 2: you can click use ai logo generator and have it generate logo's for you ( 2 generations every 24 hrs )

  Alternatively you can click quick AI launch. and simply enter information about your project for it to launch.
  can be as simple as "launch a token"
    any other information will be used to create your AI generated token specific to the information you provide.
    ideas for information to provide you can be as detailed as you want.
  Click the socials button to add social links.

Finally you can also open Toly AI chatbox and ask it to create a token for you. again being as detailed as you like and you can include but do not have to include
    - name
    - ticker
    - colors
    - theme    
    if you have socials made include them so they can be linked to token
      - website
      - telegram
      - twitter

  2) buying / selling tokens from a project launched
    goto https://dapp.solforgeai.com
  search for find and click on the projects icon to enter its menu
  in the trade menu choose how much you want to spend and click buy token
  if selling choose how much you want to sell and click sell token

3) connecting your wallet is as easy as clicking connect wallet button
  choosing your wallet of choice
  and signing the message in your wallet. ( this confirms your account to your user profile on our site. ) 
      ** no funds are transfered, no permissions given. you are simply signing a message proving ownership of your account. **
  Swap accounts by simply changing accounts in your wallet. choose the wallet provider and sign again IF new user connection.
